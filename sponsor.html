There are currently three types of sponsorships available for ICMIE'18. All sponsors will be provided an exhibition table at the conference. The opportunities are as followed (prices are in Euros):

Platinum Sponsorship, €3,500:

  • Logo on the conference website
  • Logo in the conference schedule booklet
  • 4 free registrations for the personnel from the institute

Gold Sponsorship, €2,700:

  • Logo on the conference website
  • Logo in the conference schedule booklet
  • 2 free registrations for the personnel from the institute

Silver Sponsorship, €1,800:

  • Logo on the conference website
  • 2 free registrations for the personnel from the institute

Exhibits

There are currently two types of opportunities for an exhibit at ICMIE. They are as followed (prices are in Euros):

Table exhibit, €1500.00:

  • A table setup for the entire duration of the conference
  • 1 free registration for the personnel from the institute
  • An additional €300.00 for each accompanying person

Booth exhibit, €1500:

  • A booth setup for 1 conference day
  • 1 free registration for the personnel from the institute
  • An additional €300.00 for each accompanying person

The exhibit provides a platform for companies and institutions to present their products and distribute brochures and business cards. The exhibit also provides a unique networking opportunity with many experts and researchers in the field of multimedia and human computer interaction.

If you are interested in an exhibit at ICMIE, please do not hesitate to contact us at info@icmie.net.
