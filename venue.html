We are pleased to announce that the 7th International Conference on Mechanics and Industrial Engineering (ICMIE'18) will be held in Madrid, Spain, on August 16 - 18, 2018 at the Novotel Madrid Center.

Novotel Madrid Center

Novotel Madrid Center is located in the elegant Salamanca district. The hotel is strategically located with easy access to the airport. The rooms combine modernity and elegance, technology and furnishings. All rooms are equipped with free WIFI. At Novotel restaurant you will enjoy cuisine that has a clear focus on local produce whilst remaining loyal to the cosmopolitan spirit of Madrid.

The Novotel Madrid Center hotel is easily accessible by all means of transport. The 24-hour Airport Express bus stops just 219 yds (200 m) from the hotel. The hotel also has its own taxi rank, parking for more than 300 cars and 2 metro stops nearby.

Madrid


Madrid, Spain's central capital, is a city of elegant boulevards and expansive, manicured parks such as the Buen Retiro. It’s renowned for its rich repositories of European art, including the Prado Museum’s works by Goya, Velázquez and other Spanish masters. The heart of old Hapsburg Madrid is the portico-lined Plaza Mayor, and nearby is the baroque Royal Palace and Armory, displaying historic weaponry.

No city on earth is more alive than Madrid, a beguiling place whose sheer energy carries a simple message: this city really knows how to live.
