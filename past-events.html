Past Events

The previous event in this conference series, ICMIE, was held on August 16 - 18, 2018, in Madrid, Spain. ICMIE is one of the 4 conferences that make up the World Congress on Mechanical, Chemical, and Material Engineering (MCM). Our year-long planning and organization of several committees resulted in the conference becoming a major success. Please see below some of the highlights of the event.

Best Paper Award Winners

We would like to congratulate the following authors for winning the Best Paper Award:

  • Dr. Thomas Adams, for the paper entitled: "Role of Dimensionless Parameters in Modelling MEMS Hot-arm Actuators" ;

  • Hyo Chang Cho, for the paper entitled: "Embedded Temperature Sensor Array to Measure Distributed Heat Flux for Water Jet Cooling";

  • Dr. Nenad Kuzmanić, for the paper entitled: "Effect of Impeller Position on Crystal Growth Kinetics of Borax in Dual Axial Impeller Batch Cooling Crystallizer" ;

  • Albert Materdey, for the paper entitled: "Higher-Order Numerical Solutions of the Quarter Car Suspension Model" ;

  • Dr. Artur Miros, for the paper entitled: "Experimental Investigation of the Rigid Polyurethane Foam Granulates Thermal Properties" ;

  • Mateus Moraes, for the paper entitled: "Seeing Environmental Issues as a Source of Rare Earths" ;

  • Dr. So-Yeon Ock, for the paper entitled: "A Study of the Effectiveness on the Regional Policy by the Corporate Performance Analysis in Busan";

  • Eva Sanchez-Rexach, for the paper entitled: " Antifouling Poly(ε-Caprolactone) Surfaces For Medical Devices" ;

  • Dr. Lluís Sanmiquel, for the paper entitled: "Management of Subsidence in a Potash Mining Area" ;

  • Rajat Saxena, for the paper entitled: "Experimental Assessment of Characterised PCMs for Thermal Management of Buildings in Tropical Composite Climate" ;

  • Mustafa Safa Yilmaz, for the paper entitled: "Investigation of the High Energy Single Pulses Affect on Micro Arc Oxidation Process on Aluminium" ;
